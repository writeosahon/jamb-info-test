Faith Confessions
Dear Father , I rejoice in your word that imparts faith to my spirit,causing me to see and apprehend my inheritance in christ.I function in dominion of the spirit today,conscious that all things are mine,in the name of the Lord Jesus.Amen
I stand in faith,unshakeable and unmovable, because Christ is the solid rock on which I stand .My faith is hinged on God's infallible word , and I live a life of victory,excellence,dominion, and power.I reign , and excel every day,in Jesus'Name.Amen.
Dear Father,thank you for helping me build strong and prevailing faith through your word. Nothing is impossible unto me, and no level of success,victory and progress is unattainable for me because I live by faith in the Word, in Jesus' Name Amen.
Thank you dear Father for your infalliable word ,and its ability to make me what it talks about. I live triumphantly in , and by,the word,making progress with giants strides,and expanding on every side, in Jesus' Name,Amen
Dear Father,I thank you for imparting faith to my spirit through the word. I use my faith today by my corresponding actions and verbal expressions, in line with your infalliable and eternal Word. Thus ,I live above the adversary and all adversities of life,in Jesus' Name.Amen
Dear heavenly Father, thank you for showing me that i am not helpless,but empowered to effect changes in the circumstances of life by faith and in the Name of Jesus.My faith in you and in the power of your Word prevails today, and always, and i rejoice,in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ.Amen
I live by the Word,therefore i am flourishing like a tree,planted by the rivers of water; my faith is effective. and everything I do prospers. I have given myself wholly to the Word,therefore my life is piloted by the spirit of God in the direction of God's destiny for me, in Jesus' Name
Dear Heavenly Father, I live in absolute mastery because my faith is the victory that overcomes the world. I relinquish my care and worries unto you,for i am convinced you are able to do exceedingly above what I could ever ask or think, in Jesus' Name.Amen
My faith is alive and strong,prevailing over circumstances.I refuse to stagger at the word of God through unbelief; but i am strong in faith,convinced that God's Word never fails,confident that i have whatsoever i desire when i pray,in Jesus' Name.Amen
I’m fully equipped and fortified with the whole armour of God, strong in the Lord and in the power of His might. My faith is a weapon of defence against the fiery darts of the wicked, and an instrument of victory over and against the adversary and adversities. I’m eternally triumphant in Christ. Blessed be God!
