Soul Winning
Dear Father, I thank you for the honour of making me your partner in winning others. I understand my purpose on earth: to be your light , a minister of reconciliation , pointing men in the direction of your grace to receive salvation .You've made me the dispenser of your glory and righteousness.Thank you for this calling,in Jesus' Name.Amen
Dear Father , I thank you for counting me faithful and worthy to be your partner in the ministry of reconciliation. I thank you for your grace that works in me to teach others and minister your truth , to the end that they might do the same,in Jesus' Name. Amen
I have received increased grace and ability to fulfil the ministry of reconciliation to those in my world, and in the regions beyond. I am fervent in soul-winning and intercession ; through me, the light of the glorious Gospel of Christ shines brightly in the hearts of men , destroying the shackles of bondage and darkness , and lighting up in them passion for Christ and His righteousness, in Jesus' Name.Amen
I am a new creating in Christ, born into God's glory. I dwell in safety, and I am shielded from the insecurity,economic troubles, disappointments,poverty,anger,frustration and discomfort in this world.I am in Christ , and he is my defence and salvation. Hallelujah!
I belong in the fraternity of the God-kind, having been born into God’s class, with His divine life in my spirit. I’m indestructible, impregnable, and always victorious. I’ve been translated from the ordinary realm of mankind to the supernatural class of the God-kind. Hallelujah!
Dear Father, I thank you for I’ve received the gift of righteousness; therefore, I reign in life, over and above the world’s system that condemns. I have right standing in your presence because every form of guilt and condemnation has been taken away, and I’ve received of your nature of righteousness, and walk accordingly, in Jesus’ Name. Amen
Dear Father, I thank you for having ordained me to preach the unsearchable riches of Christ. I recognize that I've been charged with the responsibility to minister life, and the power of God to the lost and hurting in this world. The light of the glorious Gospel shines intensely in their hearts, destroying the shackles of darkness. Many are snatched from the thraldom of darkness into your glorious kingdom, in Jesus' Name. Amen
Dear Father, I thank you, for I’ve received abundance of grace and of the gift of righteousness; therefore, I’m reigning in life, over and above the corrupting influences of this world: sickness, lack, and everything that limits. The grace of God is multiplied in my life through the knowledge of the Word, in Jesus’ Name. Amen
