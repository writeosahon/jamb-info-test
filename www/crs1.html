UTME candidates preparing to write Christian Religious Studies in the forthcoming UTME should note that they should read only the 4 topics and 52 subtopics listed below. The topics are;

1. CREATION TO THE DIVISION OF THE KINGDOM

This is the first topic and it has 8 subtopics. They include;

  1. A MAN AFTER GOD’S HEART
  2. DECISION MAKING
  3. DIVINE PROVIDENCE, GUIDANCE AND PROTECTION
  4. LEADERSHIP QUALITIES
  5. OBEDIENCE AND DISOBEDIENCE
  6. PARENTAL RESPONSIBILITY
  7. THE COVENANT
  8. THE SOVEREIGNTY OF GOD

2. DIVISION OF THE KINGDOM TO THE RETURN FROM EXILE AND THE PROPHETS

This is the second topic in the UTME syllabus for CRS and it has 9 subtopics. They include;

  1. CONCERN FOR JUDAH
  2. FAITH, COURAGE AND PROTECTION
  3. GOD’S MESSAGE TO NINEVEH
  4. GREED AND ITS EFFECTS
  5. HOLINESS AND DIVINE CALL
  6. PUNISHMENT AND HOPE
  7. RELIGIOUS REFORMS IN JUDAH
  8. SOCIAL JUSTICE, TRUE RELIGION AND DIVINE LOVE
  9. THE SUPREMACY OF GOD

3. SELECTED EPISTLES

This is the third topic in the JAMB syllabus for CRS and it has 16 subtopics. They include;

  1. CHRISTIAN GIVING
  2. CHRISTIAN LIVING IN THE COMMUNITY
  3. CHRISTIANS AS JOINT HEIRS WITH CHRIST
  4. CIVIC RESPONSIBILITY
  5. CORRUPTION
  6. DIGNITY OF LABOUR
  7. EFFECTIVE PRAYER
  8. FORGIVENESS
  9. HUMILITY
  10. IMPARTIALITY
  11. JUSTIFICATION BY FAITH
  12. NEW LIFE IN CHRIST
  13. SEXUAL IMMORALITY
  14. SPIRITUAL GIFTS
  15. THE LAW AND GRACE
  16. THE SECOND COMING OF CHRIST

4. THE FOUR GOSPELS AND THE ACTS OF THE APOSTLES

This is the second topic in the CRS Jamb syllabus it has nineteen (19) subtopics. They include;

  1. DISCIPLESHIP
  2. FELLOWSHIP IN THE EARLY CHURCH
  3. JESUS’ TEACHINGS ABOUT HIMSELF
  4. LOVE
  5. MIRACLES
  6. MISSION OF THE DISCIPLES
  7. MISSION TO THE GENTILES
  8. OPPOSITION TO THE GOSPEL MESSAGE
  9. RESURRECTION, APPEARANCES AND ASCENSION OF JESUS
  10. SERMON ON THE MOUNT
  11. THE BAPTISM AND TEMPTATION OF JESUS CHRIST
  12. THE BIRTH AND EARLY LIFE OF JESUS
  13. THE GREAT CONFESSION
  14. THE HOLY SPIRIT AND THE MISSION OF THE CHURCH
  15. THE LAST SUPPER
  16. THE PARABLES
  17. THE TRANSFIGURATION
  18. THE TRIALS AND THE DEATH OF JESUS
