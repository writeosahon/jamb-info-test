Righteousness
I have been recreated in true righteousness and holiness ; in the very image and likeness of God. I am walking and living in my new nature,giving vent to the will and nature of the Father, establishing His righteousness in my world. His glory , excellence, wisdom and goodness are expressed through me , in Jesus' Name.Amen
Dear Father, I thank you for the extraordinary authority vested in me , to rule and reign as a king in life. I am a dispenser of your goodness; I manifest your glory,splendour and wisdom ; your righteousness is expressed through me today, and always in Jesus' Name.Amen
Dear Father, I thank you for the oneness I have with you! Being justified by faith; acquitted and declared righteous,d I have peace with you;your righteousness  has produced peace in me,and for me in your presence;peace with prosperity! I revel in the divine harmony,bliss and Joy that you have brought me into. Thank you for this awesome priviledge, in Jesus' Name.Amen
Dear Father, I thank you for the effect of righteousness in my spirit. I am established in righteousness , therefore , I am far from oppression and from terror. Nothing is impossible to me ,for i walk in the might, dominion and liberty of the Spirit, in Jesus' Name.Amen
Dear Lord, I thank you for the path of greatness you have prepared for me to take in life which you prearranged, manifesting your glory,and expressing your righteousness ,in Jesus' Name.Amen
My life is a testimony of God's grace.His mercies and compassion surround me every day! I walk in His glory and perfection today,manifesting His righteousness,and exercising the dominion of the spirit,in Jesus' Name.Amen
