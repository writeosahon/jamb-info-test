CRS SCHEME

CHRISTIAN RELIGIOUS STUDIES

 

(May not be taken with Islamic Religious Studies and /or

West African Traditional Religion)

 

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

 

At the end of the three year course of study, the candidate will be expected to;

 

  1. have knowledge, understanding and appreciation of the religious, cultural, historical and social background of the biblical materials prescribed to be studied;

 

  1. understand the nature of biblical moral lessons in the selected passages/themes from the Bible and the extent of their relevance and possible application to present day social, political, religious and cultural life and existence;

 

  1. identify difficulties involved in the application of healthy attitudes, concepts and skills acquired from the Bible in ensuring peaceful co-existence and national development;

 

demonstrate adequate preparation for higher education through critical thinking and independent work.
