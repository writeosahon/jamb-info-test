GENERAL ENTRY REQUIREMENTS
The entry requirements which must be meet by Candidates seeking admission into First Degree, Nigeria Certificate in Education(NCE), National Innovation Diploma(NID), National Diploma(ND) programmes in Nigerian Universities and other Degree-Awarding Institutions, Polytechnics, Monotechnics, Innovative Enterprise Institutions and Colleges of Education are as follows : five (5) O'Level Credit passes including Mathematics and English Language. Complete information is available in the eBrochure which would be given every Candidate at the point of registration in every accredited CBT centres and JAMB State-Offices.
Direct Entry
Candidates must meet at least one of the following requirements before been considered for Direct Entry admission:
(a) Must obtain O'Level credit pass in at least five (5) subjects at not more than two sittings with at
least two prescribed subjects (for each discipline) at Principal or Advanced level. No
subject shall be counted twice at both Ordinary and Advanced Level
(b) Candidates must obtain two passes (in prescribed subjects for each discipline) at the Advanced-Level
Examination of IJMB, Cambridge, JUPEB or Institute of Baccalaureate with SSCE/GCE,
NTS/NBC credits equivalents in three other subjects
(c) Candidates must obtain Passes in two major subjects in the NCE with S.S.C.E., NTC/NBC credits or equivalents in
THREE other subjects (mainly for Education Courses) and ND. For Education courses,
Education may be accepted as a third A' Level subject
(d)Candidates must obtain a Minimum of lower credit grade in National Diploma or National Innovation Diploma and
the O'Level requirements
